About Us

At Mr. Bams Car Wash, we believe in quality service, convenience, and customer satisfaction. Our mobile services bring the car wash to you, whether you're at home or at work. We understand how busy life can get, and that's why we strive to make it easy for you to keep your vehicle clean without disrupting your schedule.
Mr Bam’s Mobile Car Wash was born out of a passion for cars and a love for entrepreneurship. In 2021, Ashley Bam, Owner of the Mobile Car Wash identified a gap in the market and decided to start a Car Wash that is so convenient, that it comes right to your doorstep. The area of operations is currently focused in the Franschhoek area with plans to expand to neighbouring areas such as Paarl and Stellenbosch in the very near future. Ashley brings everything needed for a high-quality car wash, including water, a generator, all cleaning products, and a vacuum, meaning all you need to do is walk outside your home and unlock your vehicle, the rest is taken care of.
